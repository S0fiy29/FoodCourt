About

Learn More About Dataran Cendekia UiTM SHAH ALAM

Dataran Cendekia is a place where students gather to buy food and drinks. The place is located in UiTM Shah Alam

  • The food and drinks that are sold meet student prices because they are affordable.
  • A large quantity of food and has a wide variety of food.
  • The location of the place which is close to the student accommodation makes it a strategic place for them to buy food and socialize.

The location branch of UiTM Shah Alam is the main center of the branch where it has many unique privileges. One of the attractions or privileges of UiTM Shah Alam includes Dataran Cendekia
